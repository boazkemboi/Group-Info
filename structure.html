We are forming a lending group where members contribute funds and borrow small loans to support each other financially. The goal is to build a sustainable fund for our members while ensuring fair loans and growing contributions.

2. Contributions

  • Initial Contribution: KES 100 per member.
  • Weekly Contribution: KES 50 per member.

3. Loan Structure

  • Loan Starting Amount: KES 200.
  • Loan Duration: 7 days.
  • Daily Interest Rate: 2%.
  • Monthly Interest Rate: 10%.

4. Interest Rate and Growth

  • Annual Interest Rate: 25%.
  • Monthly Interest: 2.08%.
  • Daily Interest: 0.17%.

5. Loan Application Process

  • Eligibility: Members must be up-to-date with contributions.
  • Application: Submit a request via WhatsApp.
  • Approval: Based on group funds availability.

6. Repayment Process

  • 7-Day Loans: Full repayment by due date.
  • Monthly Loans: Repayment at month-end.
  • Late Payments: Additional penalty may apply.

7. Entry and Exit Policy

  • New Members: Must contribute for 2 weeks before borrowing.
  • Exiting Members: Must clear all loans before leaving.

8. Group Meetings and Record Keeping

  • Meetings: Monthly (in-person or via WhatsApp).
  • Records: Maintained by the treasurer.

9. Risk Management

  • Default Risks: Loan caps to manage funds.
  • Late Payments: KES 5 penalty per day.

10. Group Growth and Expansion

As the group fund grows, we will introduce larger loans and additional financial services.

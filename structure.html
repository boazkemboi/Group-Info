Lending Group Structure & Implementation Plan

1. Overview of the Groupâ€™s Purpose

We are forming a lending group where members contribute funds and borrow small loans to support each other financially. The goal is to build a sustainable fund for our members while ensuring each member benefits through fair loans and growing contributions.

2. Contributions

  • Initial Contribution: Each member contributes KES 100 as a starting amount.
  • Ongoing Weekly Contribution: Members contribute KES 50 weekly to grow the fund.

3. Loan Structure

  • Loan Starting Amount: KES 200 for the first round.
  • Loan Duration: 7-day repayment period.
  • Daily Interest Rate: 2% (e.g., KES 4 per day for a KES 200 loan).
  • Monthly Interest Rate: 10% (e.g., KES 20 for a KES 200 loan over a month).

4. Interest Rate and Growth

  • Annual Interest Rate Target: 25%
  • Monthly Interest Equivalent: 2.08%
  • Daily Interest Equivalent: 0.17%
  • Loan Repayment: Expected within the agreed time.

5. Loan Application Process

  • Eligibility: Must have contributed initially and be up-to-date with weekly payments.
  • Application: Via WhatsApp message to the group treasurer.
  • Approval: Based on available group funds and borrowing history.

6. Repayment Process

  • Daily Loans: Full repayment due in 7 days.
  • Monthly Loans: Full repayment at month-end (installments possible).
  • Default Policy: Late payments may incur penalties.

7. Entry and Exit Policy

  • New Members: Must pay KES 100 and contribute weekly for 2 weeks before borrowing.
  • Exit: Members must clear debts before leaving.
  • Exit Penalty: Unpaid loans may result in contribution deductions.

8. Group Meetings and Record Keeping

  • Meetings: Monthly, via WhatsApp or in-person.
  • Record Keeping: Treasurer maintains all financial records and shares updates.

9. Risk Management

  • Default Risks: Loan caps based on fund size.
  • Late Payments: KES 5 per day penalty.
  • Loan Caps: Initially KES 500, may increase based on repayment history.

10. Group Growth and Expansion

  • Increase loan amounts as the fund grows.
  • Introduce new financial services.

Next Steps

  • Initial Meeting: Discuss structure and elect officials.
  • Weekly Contributions: Begin weekly payments.
  • Loan Applications: Open once funds reach KES 2,000.

Conclusion

This lending group aims to provide sustainable financial support through fair contributions and loans. Regular participation and timely repayments will help us grow this initiative.
